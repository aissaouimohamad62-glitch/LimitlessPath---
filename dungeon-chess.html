📜 Chessboard Protocols & Elite Strategies

1. Control the Center of the Board

Control the central squares (e4, d4, e5, d5) early in the opening. Whoever controls the center commands more space to maneuver and deliver decisive blows, while preventing the opponent from freely developing their pieces.

2. Rapid Development and Castling

Do not leave your king in the center to be vulnerable to attacks. Develop minor pieces (knights and bishops) quickly, and castle to protect the king and connect the rooks for a counter-attack.
